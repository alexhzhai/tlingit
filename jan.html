Cecilia Kunz was Jan’s adopted naa tlaa or clan mother. She was a very important tradition bearer and spokespearson for the L'uknax.adi (Coho) clan.


She had many Tlingit names, and Stan Tlaa was just one of them.


1910 - 2004


Janet Lee Ketchum Steinbright was born in 1941 in Juneau, AK.


Her native name is Stan Tlaa, which translates to “outside mother.” Jan jokingly wonders if this name means someone who likes to be outdoors (which she says is true) or if it means someone who is out of the clan.


In 1983, she was adopted by a Tlingit clan. Tlingit clans will usually adopt people who work for and help them. Jan had been giving workshops (on basketry and weaving) in Haines, and the tribe took notice.


Jan was adopted by two yanwáat (elders) of the clan, Cecilia Kunz (Stan Tlaa) and Lillian Hammond.


From her adoption, Jan was given these crests: she is from the Raven moiety, the Coho clan, and her house is the Diginaa Hit (the frog).


In 1995, Jan married a Tlingit man, Paul Manson Jackson.


b. 1941


When Jan and Paul married each other, both were part of the Raven moiety. In Tlingit culture, marriages are normally between people of opposite moities (ravens with eagles and eagles with ravens).


Their marriage confused some of their peers and sometimes brought unwarranted judgement at social gatherings like potlatches (traditional ceremonial feasts).


Jan recounts that, when they decided to get married, Paul asked if she would get redopted and become eagle. Jan said she was adopted raven long before she knew him and felt a strong connection with keeping her Raven identity.


Nowadays, people are marrying more in the same moiety, and the rule of marriage between raven and eagle is less strict. Jan attributes this to it now being “contemporary times.”


Dixon Scott Steinbright was born in 1971 in Oahu, HI.


His native name is Skeenya, because he reminded his clan of a particularly tall, skinny basketball player.


In the Steinbright family, the men take the first initial of their first name and go by their middle name which is a family name in my family from Scotland. (Scott's father is L.
